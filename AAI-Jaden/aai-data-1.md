1. A business can grow without tracking anything. A business can grow without tracking anything but it usually can’t scale or stay profitable for long without it.

2. Follower count is the best way to measure success. Follower count can indicate popularity but it’s not the best way to measure success for most businesses.

3. Customer opinions matter more than sales numbers. Customer opinions explain the experience while sales numbers confirm whether the business is succeeding.

4. A good decision can be made with no data. Good decisions can be made without data but data usually increases the chances of making the right decision.

5. Data is always fair. Data can be powerful but it must be carefully collected and analyzed to avoid bias.
